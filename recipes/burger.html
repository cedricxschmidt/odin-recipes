A recipe for a delicious burger



Ingredients

  • Some big burger buns
  • Good old vegetarian burger patties to your liking
  • big fat EGG
  • A bundle of fresh salat
  • TO-MA-TOS
  • some dressing, e.g. american burger sauce
  • SALT AND PEPPER ELSE IT TASTES LIKE NOTHING

How to cook

  1. Heat up the oven to 200Â°C and throw the burger buns in there for 5minutes till they're brown
  2. Heat up the pan with some olive oil on middle heat
  3. get your patty in there till it's golden and crusty
  4. halfway through, throw your egg in there
  5. take out the burger buns and prepare them with sauce, salat, tomatoes
  6. throw the patty and egg in there
  7. enjoy ur meal
